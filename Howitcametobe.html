The Process

    -In May of 1787 in Philadelphia in secrecy delegates from 12 of the 13 states got together in Phillidelphia to begin redesigning the government

    -They were called the Constitutional Convention and they started working on creating a new Constitution

    -The first draft had a system of checks and balance and included an executive branch, a representitive legislature, and a federal judiciary.

    -The Constitution initially did not include a bill of individuals rights

    -It took 4 years before the new governments form would be completed

    -Federalists felt the Bill of Rights wasnt necessary, and the Anti-Federalists felt the Constitution couldnt be without one.

    -The Bill of Rights was inspired by Thomas Jefferson, and wrote by James Madison and on December 15, 1791 the Bill of Rights became law.
